About Amani Place

Kuhusu Amani Place

Amani Place is a professional business centre in the heart of Dar es Salaam's commercial district — ideal for companies, startups, consultancies, and offices.

Amani Place ni kituo cha kitaalamu cha biashara katikati mwa eneo la biashara la Dar es Salaam — kinachofaa kwa kampuni, biashara zinazoanza, ushauri, na ofisi.

It's an "A-grade" office building offering modern workspace solutions without needing to sign long leases. With 9-12 floors of premium office space, we provide the perfect environment for businesses to thrive.

Ni jengo la ofisi la "daraja A" linalotoa ufumbuzi wa kisasa wa nafasi ya kazi bila ya kuhitaji kutia saini mikataba mirefu. Kwa sakafu 9-12 za nafasi bora za ofisi, tunatoa mazingira kamili kwa biashara kufanikiwa.

Our facilities include air-conditioned workspaces, high-speed elevators, meeting rooms, business lounge areas, and professional reception services.

Vifaa vyetu vinajumuisha maeneo ya kazi yenye ubaridi, lifti za kasi, vyumba vya mikutano, maeneo ya kupumzika ya biashara, na huduma za kitaalamu za mapokezi.

Open Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM, we're here to support your business needs with premium amenities and a prestigious address.

Wazi Jumatatu hadi Ijumaa, saa 3 asubuhi hadi saa 11 jioni, tupo hapa kusaidia mahitaji ya biashara yako kwa vivutio vya hali ya juu na anua ya heshima.
